c) 1,000 tonnes

a) Coral reefs

c) Mangroves

a) It has no impact on weather and climate

c) It causes extreme weather events

a) They emit CO2
b) They convert CO2 into bioactive metabolites
c) They absorb CO2 and store it in the atmosphere
